Frequently Asked Question





Q. What is Tomato Safety?

Tomato is an app for your mobile which gives you alerts in the case your phone gets stolen by someone.


Q. What does it do exactly?

Tomato monitors SIM swaps in your phone. An unauthorised SIM swap is reported to the two numbers you added earlier via SMS. You can also track your phone via SMS siren feature (described below). You also get location updates in case an unauthorised SIM change is attempted.


Q. I lost my phone somewhere. How to track it?

Send a text to your phone "track (last 4 characters of your password)". If your phone is not switched off and is connected to internet, you will soon get back location updates to the "superhero numbers" you have added earlier. If you believe your phone has not been stolen and is lying somewhere near around you, try "siren whistle blower" feature.



Q. What is different in Tomato?

To start with, the features which makes Tomato different is that it is super light - doesn't take a big bite from your phone's memory, processor or battery.



Q. There was something written as "siren whistle blower" in one of the above questions. What is it?

Suppose you misplaced your phone in your home or somewhere around you. You can try to ring your phone to  find it. Brilliant!!! But what if it is in silent mode?? Here, Tomato comes handy. Send a SMS "siren (last 4 characters of your password)". No matter if your phone is in silent mode or not, your phone will give a loud siren sound.



Q. Does Tomato spy on me?

No, not at all. We can assure you that we are not the "bad guys". We do not collect any user identifying information.



Q. What is the use of this app if my phone never gets stolen??

You can catch your friends when they play prank on you by hiding your phone. In that situation the siren feature of the app comes handy.



Q. I was thinking of changing my SIM card... Will Tomato flag that too ??

You should turn off the Tomato protection before doing that. After putting in the new SIM you can turn on the protection. Please note that if you are using a dual SIM phone the position of SIM cards do matter.
